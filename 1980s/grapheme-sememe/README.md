# Grapheme-sememe synthetic word reading

**Source:** Hinton & Sejnowski (1986).
**Demonstrates:** Distributed representations are damage-resistant; relearning is fast; retraining 18 of 20 associations spontaneously restores the other 2.

30 grapheme units (3 letter positions x 10 letters) -> 20 hidden -> 30 sememe units. 20 random grapheme->sememe mappings. Used for the famous lesion / relearning / spontaneous-recovery experiments.
