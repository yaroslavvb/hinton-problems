# RNN pathological long-term-dependency tasks

**Source:** Sutskever, Martens, Dahl & Hinton (2013), "On the importance of initialization and momentum in deep learning", ICML. (Original tasks from Hochreiter & Schmidhuber.)
**Demonstrates:** Long-term dependency optimization with proper initialization and momentum.

Tasks: addition, multiplication, XOR, temporal-order, 3-bit / random-permutation memorization, noiseless memorization.
